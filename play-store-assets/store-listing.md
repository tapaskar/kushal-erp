## Full Description (max 4000 chars)
KushalRWA is a comprehensive management application designed for Residential Welfare Associations (RWAs) and housing societies. It provides a unified platform for staff operations, governance workflows, procurement approvals, and society management.

KEY FEATURES:

Multi-Role Access
- Society Admin, Estate Manager, and RWA Body members
- Staff roles: Security, Housekeeping, Supervisor, Maintenance, and more
- Residents can view staff work and society updates
- Configurable permissions per role per society

NFA Procurement Workflow
- Create Notes for Approval with L1/L2/L3 vendor quotes
- Multi-level approval: Executive members vote (50% quorum)
- Treasurer approval after executive quorum is met
- Automated Purchase Order creation
- PDF export with full approval timeline
- Share via WhatsApp, email, or save

Shift Management
- Clock in and clock out with GPS verification
- Geofence-based campus presence validation
- Optional selfie verification at check-in
- Complete shift history with hours worked

Task Management
- View and manage assigned tasks
- Priority-based task organization (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)
- Task status tracking (Pending, In Progress, Completed)
- Photo evidence for task completion

Security & Patrol
- Checkpoint-based patrol routes
- Real-time patrol progress tracking
- Incident reporting with GPS location
- Emergency SOS alerts to supervisors

Housekeeping
- Daily cleaning schedules by zone
- Before/after photo documentation
- Supply request management
- Cleaning completion verification

Inventory Management
- Item search and barcode scanning
- Quantity tracking and location info
- Inventory audit support

Visitor Management
- Visitor check-in/check-out logging
- Visitor type categorization
- Contact and purpose tracking

ROLE-BASED ACCESS:
The app automatically shows relevant features based on your role. Society admins can configure which modules and permissions each role has through the web dashboard.

PRIVACY FIRST:
- Location tracking requires explicit consent
- Location data auto-deletes after 90 days
- Consent can be revoked anytime from the app
- No data shared with third parties

Built for Indian residential societies with a focus on reliability, ease of use, and privacy.
